Venice One Day Tour

CommandZ Sep. 15th 763

Accommodation:

It is recommended to live in the hotel reget near the mestre railway station. It is inexpensive and air-conditioned. It is close to the train. The nearby Chinese Lamian Noodles restaurant is also delicious

Journey:

It is recommended to leave early to avoid exposure to sunlight, and there are not many tourists, making it easy to take photos. At 9 o'clock in the morning, we went to Gondola at the Arito Bridge, and we bargained to a ship for 60 Euros. After taking the boat, I went to Rainbow Island by boat. The round-trip transportation took two hours, but there were no special check-in points for the colorful houses. There were many small shops on the island, and I went to the restaurant recommended by Xiaohongshu, but only received cash. Afternoon: Sunken Ship Bookstore - Bridge of Sighs - St. Martin's Square and Church, you can choose to climb the top. In fact, it will be finished soon. There are so many people in Bridge of Sighs that it is not easy to make movies. You can choose to sit down at a bar and take a break after browsing, but some shops on the main island only accept cash. You can also choose to visit some souvenirs
