The most beautiful tomb in the world

Sanjay Gandhi Mar. 18th 425

Where:

The capital of Uttar Pradesh, India. It takes 2 hours to get here by train from the capital New Delhi.

Recommended Travel time:

3 days

The Taj Mahal is a typical Muslim tomb building. The Regular tetrahedron structure is symmetrical in the center and protected by stone pillars. The outer facade of the tomb is decorated with various gemstones to create scriptures from the Quran. The size of the scriptures is designed to be large and small, and when people look up and recite, they will feel the consistency between the top and bottom. The four stone pillars tilt outward at a slight angle, so that in the event of an earthquake, they will not collapse inward and damage the tomb. The best viewing angle of the Taj Mahal is from the remote viewing platform. The close view is a flower bed composed of lilies and roses. The middle view is two rows of evergreen trees. A straight water belt is sandwiched between the trees. Sitting on the distant platform is the most beautiful tomb in the world. Entering the main entrance of the tomb, two stone coffins of the king and Taj were placed in the center. They can finally snuggle together and never part.
